Let's find out if there's a fit

Contact

Most of the right conversations start the same way: someone has a problem they haven't been able to fully articulate, and 30 minutes of structured conversation gets them there. That's the goal.

Who This Is For

I respond to everyone who reaches out with a real inquiry. I don't respond to cold outreach from recruiters for roles that don't match this profile, generic partnership pitches, or requests to "pick my brain" without a stated agenda.

If you're not sure whether your situation qualifies — reach out anyway. The worst outcome is I suggest someone better suited to your problem.

Inquiry type 01

Fractional / Advisory / Consulting

Exploring whether there's a fit for a fractional, advisory, or consulting engagement. Describe the company, the stage, and the problem. I'll tell you quickly whether it's in my zone.

Inquiry type 02

Senior Full-Time Roles

VP Product, Head of Product, CPO — if the company, stage, and problem match. Include the company, role scope, and why you think there's a fit. Recruiters: include the client name.

Inquiry type 03

Speaking & Panels

Product strategy, AI product development, product operating models, and scaling product organizations. Describe the event, audience, and what you're hoping attendees walk away with.

Inquiry type 04

Venture Conversations

If you're building something and think there's a reason to explore involvement beyond a standard advisory relationship — equity, co-founder, operating role. Be specific about what you're building and what you need.

How I Approach the Work

The operating principles behind the outcomes.

Principle 01

Shipping is easy. Scaling is the work.

But once proper organizational structure in place, scale is inevitable.

Principle 02

Instrumentation before roadmap.

Quality decisions are impossible without proper data.

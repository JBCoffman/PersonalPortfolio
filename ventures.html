Other companies & initiatives

Ventures

Alongside client work, I'm involved in building a small set of other companies and initiatives. These are complementary — not distractions.

Context

The fractional product work is the primary offering — that's where I spend most of my time and attention, and it's what this site is primarily built around. The ventures listed here are separate from that work in scope, but connected to it in intent.

Some of these are companies I've co-founded or hold equity in. Some are initiatives I support in an advisory or operational capacity. Some are earlier-stage bets. All of them are things I'd be comfortable putting my name on.

I don't operate a holding company or a portfolio — this is a small, intentional set of projects. I keep them visible here for transparency, not to complicate the primary narrative.

What this page is not

This isn't a founder directory, a VC portfolio, or a side-hustle showcase. It's a clean acknowledgment that the work extends beyond direct client engagements — and that those extensions are worth being transparent about.

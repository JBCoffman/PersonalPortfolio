# Personal Product Executive Website Blueprint

## Site Goal
Create a personal website that primarily converts for:
- contract / consulting engagements
- fractional product leadership work
- advisory opportunities

And secondarily supports:
- VP Product / Head of Product / CPO candidacy
- credibility for the user's broader company ecosystem

## Positioning Principle
This site should be **consulting-first, executive-caliber, and recruiter-safe**.

Primary narrative:
- Senior product executive available for fractional, advisory, and select consulting engagements.

Secondary narrative:
- Also involved in building and supporting other ventures.

The site should **not** read like a generic PM portfolio, resume dump, founder directory, or holding-company homepage.

# Global Copy Strategy

## Tone
- executive
- commercially sharp
- credible
- concise
- direct
- not overly self-promotional

## Messaging Priorities
1. Business outcomes over responsibilities
2. Clarity over cleverness
3. Trust over hype
4. Seniority without sounding detached
5. Specificity around problems solved

## Core Themes
- product strategy tied to business results
- revenue and retention impact
- scaling product organizations
- AI product and monetization opportunities
- platform / operating model maturity
- cross-functional leadership

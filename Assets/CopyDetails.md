# Content Rules For Final Messaging Phase

When writing the final copy later:

## Must do
- lead with outcomes
- sound senior and commercially literate
- keep sentences tight
- make every section earn its space
- let consulting remain primary
- make executive candidacy feel natural, not bolted on

## Must avoid
- generic PM portfolio phrasing
- startup clichés
- “passionate product leader” style language
- overlong autobiography
- overemphasizing side ventures above the main offer
- sounding like a holding company directory
